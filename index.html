About me

The person
behind the work.

I'm Riyadh Hussain, a 19-year-old civil engineering student at Girijananda Choudhury University, Guwahati, Assam. But honestly my curiosity doesn't stop at concrete and structures, barely even starts there.

I got into video editing during my 3-year diploma at Nagaon Polytechnic, started freelancing with CapCut, and that spark turned into a genuine love for visual storytelling, creative approach and making things that didn't exist before.

Schooled at Kendriya Vidyalaya Nagaon. Certified in AutoCAD 2D&3D and Revit Architecture. Outside of that, I draw, I dance, I build stuff out of scrap and leftover materials just to see what happens. I also have what some might call extremely strong opinions about films and music, which my friends find either impressive or exhausting, depending on the day.

What drives me: I'm a quick learner who thrives when the work is hard and the standard is high. I haven't mastered everything yet, that's kind of the whole point.
